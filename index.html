Suplementos Yerba Buena

Summary

In this project I performed manual functional testing for a sports supplements store built on Tiendanube, the leading e-commerce platform in Latin America. The client needed to ensure the site worked as expected and that no details were overlooked.

For this purpose, I used Jira and Xray for managing and tracking the testing process.

With the creation and execution of an effective test suite, both for verifying key components and evaluating the overall details of the site, I managed to deliver the client comprehensive testing results and valuable feedback on areas where quality could be improved.

Key responsabilities

  • Configuring Jira project and Xray test management plugin in order to plan, design, execute and track tests, as well as generate test reports.
  • Writing test cases and grouping them into cohesive test suites according to the sections and functionalities of the site.
  • Executing test cases and documenting the process with screenshots and gifs.
  • Writing issue reports.
  • Documenting the testing process results in a summary for the client.

Special thanks to Gustavo from Suplementos Yerba Buena for trusting in my work!
